Linguine

News and Politics on Your Schedule

Version 0.2.0

Linguine is an app that provides immediate access to the latest podcasts from several providers of news and opinions. Keep up to date with what is going on around the world! For more information, please visit the Linguine website.

This is a free and open-source program. However, if you have enjoyed it, please consider donating $1 (or whatever amount you feel appropriate). These funds will help me to continue working on this and other apps. Thank you!
